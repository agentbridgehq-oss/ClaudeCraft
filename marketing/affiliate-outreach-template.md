# Affiliate / Creator Outreach Template

**Who to send this to:** people who already talk to your exact buyer segments — productivity YouTubers/TikTokers, freelance/solopreneur Substack writers, career-advice or resume-tips creators, study/college-life content accounts, "AI tips" accounts. Look for creators with genuine, engaged (not huge) audiences over big-follower accounts with low engagement — research showed niche-fit beats raw reach for this kind of conversion.

**Before sending anything:** read a few of their recent posts so the message can reference something real and specific. A generic copy-paste outreach message gets ignored or marked as spam — these only work personalized.

**Commission structure to offer:** 25% of the sale price per referral (store credit/cash via whatever payment method is easiest for you to track manually at this stage — PayPal works fine for low volume before you need real affiliate software).

---

## Template — Email or DM

Subject (if email): Quick one — partnership idea for [their content topic]

Hi [Name],

I've been following [specific thing — their channel/newsletter/account] for a bit, and your [specific recent post/video] on [topic] was genuinely useful — [one specific honest thing you liked about it, not generic flattery].

I built ClaudeCraft — done-for-you Claude AI skill bundles for [their audience type, e.g. "freelancers/solo business owners"]. Each bundle is a set of ready-to-paste Claude prompts + a real setup walkthrough, $19-$49, one-time purchase, 30-day guarantee. [Site link]

I think it'd genuinely be useful to your audience specifically because [one honest, specific reason tied to their content — not generic].

If you're open to it, I'd love to set you up as an affiliate — 25% commission on anything that comes through your link, no minimum, paid out [monthly via PayPal / whatever you decide]. Happy to also just send you a free copy of whichever bundle fits your audience best, no strings, if you want to actually try it before deciding anything.

Either way, really do appreciate the [specific thing again] — no pressure either way on the partnership.

[Your name]

## Follow-Up (if no response after ~1 week)

Hi [Name] — no worries if this isn't a fit, just wanted to bump this in case it got buried. Happy to just send a free bundle either way if useful for [specific audience reason]. No need to reply if not interested!

---

## Tracking (manual, before you need real affiliate software)

For each creator, give them a unique discount code (e.g. `SARAH20` for 20% off their audience, set up directly in Stripe as a coupon) instead of a generic link — this lets you see exactly how many sales came from each creator in the Stripe dashboard without needing separate affiliate tracking software yet. Pay out commission based on what that code actually generated.

**Abuse note from the research:** cap how many times any single code can be redeemed if you're worried about it leaking onto public coupon sites, and don't promise huge guaranteed commissions upfront to unverified creators — pay based on actual tracked redemptions only.
