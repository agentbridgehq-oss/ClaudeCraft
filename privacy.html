Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026
This describes exactly what ClaudeCraft collects and why — no more, no less. We do not sell your data, and we do not run third-party advertising trackers on this site.

DataWhenWhy

We do not run Google Analytics, ad pixels, or any third-party advertising tracker on this site as of this writing.

2. How Support Requests Are Handled

When you email support@claudecraft.ca, an AI system reviews the message to either answer simple questions directly or flag it for a human teammate to handle personally — this applies to anything involving money (refunds, discounts) or anything the AI isn't fully confident about. The AI never completes a refund or discount on its own; a human always reviews and executes those.

3. Third Parties We Use

  • Stripe & PayPal — payment processing. See their respective privacy policies for how they handle payment data.
  • Resend — our email delivery provider, used to send your purchase confirmation and download links.
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — powers our on-site AI chat guide and support-email triage. Messages you send to our chat widget or support inbox may be processed by Anthropic's API to generate a response. Anthropic's own privacy practices govern that processing on their end.

We do not share your email address with any of these parties beyond what's needed for them to perform their specific function (e.g., Resend needs your email to deliver your download links).

4. How Long We Keep Data

Purchase and support records are kept as long as reasonably needed to honor our 30-day guarantee, respond to follow-up questions, and meet basic bookkeeping obligations. Newsletter subscribers are kept until you unsubscribe or request removal.

5. Your Rights

You can ask us, at any time, to:

  • Tell you what data we have on file for your email address
  • Delete your data (note: this may affect our ability to verify a future refund request tied to that purchase)
  • Unsubscribe from the newsletter (also available via the unsubscribe link in any newsletter email)

Email support@claudecraft.ca for any of the above — a real person handles these requests.

6. Children's Privacy

ClaudeCraft is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

If our data practices change in a meaningful way, we'll update this page and the "Last updated" date above.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email support@claudecraft.ca.
