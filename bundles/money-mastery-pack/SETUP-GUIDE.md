# Setup Guide — Money Mastery Pack

Total time: about 5 minutes for your first skill, then ~1 minute for each one after that.
You do not need to install any software, link a bank account, or share any real financial account numbers. Everything happens inside your web browser, in a private chat with Claude.

---

## Step 1: Open Claude

1. Go to **claude.ai** in your web browser.
2. If you don't have an account yet, click **Sign Up** and create one — it's free.
3. If you already have an account, click **Log In**.

---

## Step 2: Find "Projects"

1. Once you're logged in, look at the **left side** of the screen.
2. Click on **Projects**.
   - If you don't see "Projects" in the sidebar, click the menu icon (☰) in the top-left first to open the sidebar.

---

## Step 3: Create a New Project

1. Click the button that says **+ New Project** (or just **New Project**).
2. Name it after the skill — for example: `Budget Builder`.
3. Click **Create** (or **Create Project**).

---

## Step 4: Add the Skill

1. Inside your new project, find **Custom Instructions** (sometimes labeled **Project Instructions**).
2. Click it.
3. Open the **SKILLS.md** file that came with your download.
4. Find **Skill #1: Budget Builder** (or whichever skill you're starting with).
5. Copy the text inside the gray box — the part between the ``` marks only, not the headings around it.
6. Paste that text into the Custom Instructions box in Claude.
7. Click **Save**.

---

## Step 5: Use It

1. Start a new chat inside that project.
2. Type your numbers in plain language — there's no required format. Use the "Try it" example under each skill if you want a fast first test.
3. Claude will respond using the skill you just installed.

That project is now permanently your Budget Builder specialist. Every time you open it, Claude already knows the job.

---

## A Note on Privacy

You never need to type real account numbers, passwords, or full account details into Claude — and you shouldn't. Every skill in this pack works perfectly using rounded numbers and categories ("about $450 on groceries," not your actual bank login). Treat it like talking to a knowledgeable friend, not logging into your bank.

---

## Setting Up the Rest of Your 10 Skills

Repeat Steps 3-5 for each skill — **one skill per project**. Don't combine skills into the same project; each works best with its own dedicated, focused space.

Most customers start with whichever skill matches their most immediate money stress:
- Feeling out of control month to month → **Budget Builder**
- Carrying balances that won't move → **Debt Payoff Planner**
- A bill that feels too high → **Bill Negotiation Script Writer**
- No safety net → **Emergency Fund Planner**

There's no wrong order. Start where it actually hurts.

## Troubleshooting

**"I don't see a Custom Instructions option."**
Make sure you're inside a Project, not a regular chat. The option only appears inside Projects.

**"Claude isn't responding the way the skill describes."**
Double-check you copied the entire block of text, start to finish. A partial copy/paste is the most common cause.

**"The math doesn't match what my bank app says."**
Claude is doing the math you give it — if a number is rounded or a category is mislabeled, the output will reflect that. Try again with more precise numbers for anything important.

**"Can I edit the skill instructions?"**
Yes. These are starting points, not rigid rules — tweak the wording to fit your exact situation.

**Still stuck?** Email **support@claudecraft.ca** — we'll get you sorted, usually same day.

Claude is a powerful thinking partner. It is not a substitute for professional financial advice on major decisions.
