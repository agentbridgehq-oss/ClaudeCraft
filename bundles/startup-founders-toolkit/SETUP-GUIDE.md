# Setup Guide — Startup Founder's Toolkit

Total time: about 5 minutes for your first skill, then ~1 minute for each one after that.
You do not need to install any software. Everything happens inside your web browser.

---

## Step 1: Open Claude

1. Open your web browser and go to **claude.ai**.
2. If you don't have an account yet, click **Sign Up** — email or an existing Google account both work, and it's free.
3. Check your email for a verification link if asked, and click it to confirm.
4. If you already have an account, click **Log In** instead.
5. You're now on the main Claude chat screen.

---

## Step 2: Find "Projects"

1. Look at the **left side** of the screen.
2. Click **Projects**.
   - If you don't see it, click the menu icon (☰) in the top-left first to open the sidebar.
   - On mobile or a narrow window, tap the menu icon to expand it.

---

## Step 3: Create a New Project

1. Click **+ New Project** (or **New Project**).
2. Name it after the skill — for example: `Pitch Deck Builder`.
3. Click **Create**.
4. You're dropped into your new, empty Project.

---

## Step 4: Add the Skill

1. Inside the Project, find **Custom Instructions** (sometimes labeled **Project Instructions**).
2. Click it.
3. Open the **SKILLS.md** file from your download.
4. Find the skill you want to start with — each has its own numbered heading.
5. Select the text inside the gray code box (between the ``` marks) — click before the first word and drag to after the last, or click inside and press `Ctrl+A`.
6. Copy it with `Ctrl+C` (`Cmd+C` on Mac).
7. Switch back to Claude, click inside the Custom Instructions box, and paste with `Ctrl+V` (`Cmd+V`).
8. Confirm it pasted cleanly, start to finish.
9. Click **Save**.

---

## Step 5: Use It

1. Start a new chat inside that Project.
2. Use the "Try it" example under the skill, or paste in your own real numbers/situation.
3. Press Enter or click send.
4. Claude responds using exactly that skill — that Project is now permanently your specialist for it.

---

## Setting Up the Rest of Your 10 Skills

Repeat Steps 3-5 for each skill — one skill per Project. Most founders start with whichever matches what's actually on their plate:

- Fundraising soon → **Pitch Deck Outline Builder**, then **Cap Table & Fundraising Math Explainer**
- Already raised, reporting to investors → **Investor Update Writer**, **Board Meeting Prep Briefing**
- Pre-build, testing an idea → **MVP Validation Interview Script Writer**
- Tension with a cofounder → **Cofounder Conflict Resolution Script Writer** (don't wait on this one — small issues compound fast)
- Watching cash → **Runway & Burn Rate Calculator**, check it monthly at minimum

---

## A Note on Privacy

You never need to paste your actual bank account numbers, full legal cap table documents, or other sensitive identifiers into Claude — and you shouldn't. Rounded numbers and described scenarios ("about $180K in the bank," not your actual statement) work just as well for every skill in this pack.

---

## Troubleshooting

**"I don't see a Custom Instructions option."**
Make sure you're inside a Project, not a regular chat — it only appears inside Projects.

**"Claude isn't responding the way the skill describes."**
Double-check you copied the entire gray box, start to finish. A partial copy/paste is the most common cause.

**"The fundraising/cap table math doesn't match what my lawyer said."**
Claude does general educational math based on what you give it — always have a real startup lawyer review actual term sheets and cap table documents before signing anything.

**"Can I edit the skill instructions?"**
Yes — these are starting points. Tweak wording to fit your specific industry or stage.

**Still stuck?** Email **support@claudecraft.ca** — we'll get you sorted, usually same day.
