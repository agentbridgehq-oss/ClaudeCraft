# Setup Guide — 55+ AI Starter Kit
### Easy-Read Edition — Every Step Spelled Out

**Tip:** If this text feels small, you can make it bigger. On most computers, hold the `Ctrl` key (or `Cmd` on a Mac) and press `+` a few times. Each press makes everything on the screen larger.

You will not break anything by following these steps. If something doesn't look quite right, you can always start over. There is no wrong way to try this.

---

## Before You Start

You will need:
- A computer, tablet, or phone with internet
- A web browser (this is the program you use to look at websites — like Chrome, Safari, or Edge)
- About 10 minutes, somewhere quiet

That's everything. You do not need to download or install anything.

---

## Step 1: Go to the Claude Website

1. Open your web browser.
2. In the address bar at the top, type exactly this: **claude.ai**
3. Press the **Enter** key on your keyboard.

---

## Step 2: Create Your Free Account

1. You'll see a button that says **Sign Up**. Click it.
2. Follow the steps it gives you (usually just your email address, or signing in with a Google account).
3. This is free. You will not be asked to pay anything to create an account.

*Already have an account from before? Click **Log In** instead of Sign Up.*

---

## Step 3: Find "Projects"

1. Look at the **left edge** of your screen.
2. You should see a word that says **Projects**.
3. Click on it.

*Don't see it? Look for three small lines stacked on top of each other (☰) in the top-left corner. Click that first — it opens a hidden menu — and "Projects" will be inside it.*

---

## Step 4: Make a New Project

1. Look for a button that says **+ New Project** or just **New Project**.
2. Click it.
3. A box will appear asking you to name it. Type: `Recipe Helper`
4. Click **Create**.

---

## Step 5: Add Your First Skill

1. Inside your new project, look for **Custom Instructions** (it might say **Project Instructions**). Click it.
2. Open the file called **SKILLS.md** that you downloaded.
3. Find **Skill #1: Recipe Generator**.
4. There's a gray box with text inside it. Click at the very start of that text, then drag your mouse to the very end to select it all. (Or: click inside the box, press `Ctrl+A` to select everything, then `Ctrl+C` to copy.)
5. Go back to Claude. Click inside the empty Custom Instructions box.
6. Press `Ctrl+V` (or `Cmd+V` on a Mac) to paste.
7. Click **Save**.

**You did it.** That project will remember this skill forever — you never have to set it up again.

---

## Step 6: Try It Out

1. Inside that same project, find the place where you type a message (usually at the bottom of the screen).
2. Type something like: *"I have chicken breast, rice, and broccoli."*
3. Press **Enter** or click the send button.
4. Claude will write you a recipe using exactly what you told it.

---

## Adding More Skills Later

Whenever you're ready for another skill, just repeat **Steps 4 and 5** with a new project name and a different skill from SKILLS.md (there are 10 total). There's no rush — even using just one or two skills regularly is a great start.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I have to pay for anything?**
No. Everything in this guide uses the free version of Claude.

**What if I make a mistake?**
You can't damage anything. If something looks wrong, just delete what you pasted and try copying it again.

**What if Claude gives a strange or wrong answer?**
Just tell it what's wrong, in your own words — like "that's not quite right, can you make it simpler?" It will try again.

**Can my family help me set this up?**
Absolutely — this guide is also a great one to do together with a son, daughter, or grandchild over the phone or in person.
